Cancellation and Refund Policy

We strive to provide excellent services and customer satisfaction. This Cancellation and Refund Policy outlines the terms under which you may cancel your service and request a refund.

1. Cancellation Policy

  • Subscription Services: You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation will take effect at the end of the current billing period.
  • One-Time Services: Cancellations for one-time services must be requested within 3 days of purchase.

2. Refund Policy

  • Subscription Services: Refunds are not provided for subscription services already delivered. If you cancel, you will retain access until the end of the billing period.
  • One-Time Services: Refunds for one-time services are available if requested within 3 days of purchase, provided the service has not been delivered or started.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact us at hello@digitalteam360.com with your order details and the reason for the refund request.

4. Processing Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 3 days of receiving your request. Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Cancellation and Refund Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on our website.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Cancellation and Refund Policy, please contact us at hello@digitalteam360.com
